Foreign Languages' Teacher, All Ages

Mr Freeman, Nevinnomyssk, Russia


 My last adventure as Teacher in real. I had chance to discover true Russia. As Russian speaker, this was a long time desire : Teaching in Russia. A country where many influences can be experienced. Mr. Freeman was prologue to my next new adventure in IT Media. Communication is key of success. My students at our training center were in Elementary and Junior Schools for French class. This young generation of Russian Federation is a chance for Moscow, Their envy and enthusiasm for learning a language writing a different alphabet. I felt the friendship between Russia and France. At same time, i was teaching English for Adult and having Talking Club. The Welcome i received from all my students was very friendly. Of course students learning French and English are really different. However this diversity is a source of inspiration for any teachers in this modern world. Smiles and Games were the key of our cooperation. We exchange our pronunciation way of our languages for improving our speaking, listening and understading skills. I also had pleasure to train a Russian teacher for Chinese Class. Nostalgy is special feeling in Russia, even if before was better, the next day would make you surprise day by day!
